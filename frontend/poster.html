Context & Challenges

The energy transition requires complex management of electrical grids to integrate intermittency and optimize costs. This project models a national grid to:

  • ✔ Couple supply and demand in real-time.
  • ✔ Minimize costs via the SPOT market (Merit Order).
  • ✔ Secure the network against congestions (N-1).
3

Modeling & Resolution

The network is modeled as a directed graph G = (N, A) via NetworkX.

Resolution (Economic Dispatch) is performed by the Network Simplex algorithm:

  • Industry standard (used by RTE, CPLEX).
  • Optimizes global flows by eliminating costly cycles.
  • Integrates a dynamic penalty linked to the Carbon Tax:

C_marginal = C_base + (E_CO2 × Tax)
